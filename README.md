# ARC

Personal velocity system.

Three domains. One question. Every day.

*"Am I moving, or am I drifting?"*

---

**[Live App](https://arc-jet.vercel.app)** · **[Ethos](https://arc-jet.vercel.app/ethos.html)** · **[MVP Spec](./Arc_MVP.docx)**

---

**BODY** — Did you move today?
**BUILD** — Did you make progress?
**EXPOSURE** — Did you show up socially?

Swipe to complete. Hold to undo. The graph tells you the truth.

## Quick Start (iPhone)

1. Open **[arc-jet.vercel.app](https://arc-jet.vercel.app)** in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down → **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Name it **Arc** → tap **Add**
5. Open from your home screen — runs fullscreen, no browser bar

## Quick Start (Android)

1. Open **[arc-jet.vercel.app](https://arc-jet.vercel.app)** in Chrome
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (top right)
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
4. Name it **Arc** → tap **Add**

## Quick Start (Desktop)

Just open **[arc-jet.vercel.app](https://arc-jet.vercel.app)** in any browser. That's it.

## How It Works

**Morning** — Open Arc. Type your intent. Fill one task per domain. Close. Under 60 seconds.

**During the day** — Swipe a card to mark done. Gold fill. Card locks. Under 10 seconds.

**Midnight** — Day freezes. Whatever's done is done. Tomorrow starts blank.

**Sunday** — Tap Velocity. See your week. See which domains are strong. Adjust.
